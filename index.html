If you are here just to see my professional life, you can find my resume in Portfolio page. If not, then let me share you my life story till date.( Don’t worry I will do it in short )

I grew up in West Bengal, India. I can’t pinpoint the location because I shifted more than three cities throughout my whole childhood. I hope there are more cities in my future. I always had big dreams but no way to fulfil it. Now, I am an official adult( I am 21 when I am writing it) and I definitely want to experience and live through every one of my dreams.

I like to experience everything in my life. But one thing I love that is to travel. I feel free when I am going up the mountains or sweating in a beach somewhere. It is my " aim " to travel the whole world and i am working hard towards that goal.

I am giving you my raw story, not some professional thing for you to read.
